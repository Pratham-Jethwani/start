Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

What happens if I exceed my case limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade or purchase additional cases at ₹200 per case.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees, ever. You only pay for your monthly or annual subscription.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime with no penalties. You'll have access until the end of your billing period.
